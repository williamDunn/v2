About

Lydia Li is a Chinese composer for film and other media. She likes to create unique and emotionally-driven music that engages the listener.

She began her musical journey as a classically trained pianist and discovered a passion for writing songs and original melodies.

While attending the Communication University of Zhejiang and studying communications, she served as a host for the local radio station ‘Hangzhou MIX FM90.7’. In addition to producing music for the station’s programs, she also worked as a composer’s assistant in Shanghai, scoring several films, advertisements, and documentaries, including Materia Medical China which was broadcasted on Jiangsu satellite television.

Lydia writes in a wide range of styles, blending acoustic, synthetic, and natural sounds. She is currently pursuing a master’s degree in scoring at Columbia College Chicago and hopes to continue writing music for film, television, and other media.
